# Meta Crypto Advertising Compliance – CoinBarn Trader

## Compliance Principles
This campaign was designed to comply with Meta’s cryptocurrency advertising guidelines.

---

## What We Avoid
- Guaranteed profits
- Income or ROI promises
- Misleading urgency
- “Get rich quick” language

---

## What We Emphasize
- Educational messaging
- Tool-based positioning
- Transparency
- Early access and product development

---

## Creative Review Checklist
- Neutral chart visuals
- Clear disclaimers
- No financial claims
- Professional tone

---

## Disclaimer
This campaign is a mock project created for portfolio demonstration purposes only.
